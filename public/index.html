About Sareh

Sareh Heidari is a senior software engineer in London, UK, working with React.js. In her work, she prioritises web performance, accessibility and application resilience. She cares about inclusion in tech.

She has given talks at international conferences on a range of topics - from scalable CSS, to localisation, to closing keynotes.
